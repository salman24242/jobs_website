About the role

Trailhead Analytics helps product teams understand how their experiments actually moved the needle. We're looking for a Customer Success Lead to own onboarding, expansion, and renewals for our mid-market segment (roughly 80 accounts, $20k–$80k ARR each).

Your week, roughly
