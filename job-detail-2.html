About Lumen Health

Lumen Health builds the scheduling and triage tools used by ~120 community clinics across Germany, Austria, and the Netherlands. We're a 40-person company with a small, generalist design team of three.

About the role

You'll lead the design of our patient-facing booking experience — end-to-end, from research synthesis to ship. Expect to spend roughly a third of your time on user interviews and observational research in partner clinics, a third on shaping flows in Figma, and a third on pairing with engineering.

What we're looking for

  • 4+ years designing complex, real-world product experiences.
  • Comfort running your own qualitative research.
  • A portfolio that explains decisions, not just outputs.
  • Working German is helpful but not required; clinical staff often prefer it.

How we hire

Three conversations: an intro call, a portfolio walkthrough, and a paid take-home exercise reviewed with the team. We share salary, scope, and next steps in writing after each step.
