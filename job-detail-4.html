About Mosaic Education

Mosaic builds lightweight classroom tools — gradebooks, attendance, parent communication — used by teachers in 40+ countries. We're a 28-person team, profitable, and stubbornly opposed to growth at the expense of teacher trust.

About the role

You'll join a frontend team of four and lead a slice of our gradebook redesign. We use React, TypeScript, and a small in-house component library that values typography and accessibility over novelty.

What we're looking for

  • 3+ years of production React, ideally with a real interest in accessibility.
  • Care for layout, typography, and small interactions.
  • Comfort working with international users — English doesn't have to be your first language.

How we hire

An intro chat, a paid 4-hour take-home, and a debrief with two engineers and a teacher from our user-research panel. We share salary up front.
